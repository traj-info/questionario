EUS practice survey in Latin America

Dear colleagues,

EUS practice has been growing in the last few years; however the real status of EUS in Latin America is unknown. In view of this gap, we have designed this questionnaire with the aim of gathering information from all doctors that perform EUS in various countries in Latin America.

We count on your valuable contribution as you respond to this questionnaire.
This questionnaire will take approximately 8 minutes of your time.

NOTE: To keep all your information and answers strictly confidential, firstly, you need to register via the link below, as this will allow you to receive a password to the questionnaire in your e-mail. Click here to register.

Please respond this questionnaire only if you perform EUS.

Thank you.
